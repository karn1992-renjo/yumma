# Privacy Policy

FoodFlow collects account details, contact information, delivery addresses, location data needed for delivery, order history, payment references, support messages, and device notification tokens. We use this data to operate the marketplace, process orders and refunds, assign drivers, send service notifications, prevent abuse, and meet legal obligations.

Payment card, UPI, and banking details are handled by configured payment providers. FoodFlow stores gateway references and transaction status, not raw card data.

Customers, restaurant users, and drivers may request profile updates, account support, or data deletion through the support channel shown in the app. Some transaction records may be retained when required for accounting, fraud prevention, dispute handling, or compliance.

Production deployments must configure real contact details, jurisdiction-specific disclosures, retention periods, and subprocessors before launch.
