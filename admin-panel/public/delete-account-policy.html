foodflow Account Deletion Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Overview

This policy explains how users, restaurant partners, staff users, and delivery partners of foodflow can request deletion of their account and related personal data. It applies to foodflow apps and services operated by Adgraph Media Private Limited, including foodflow, foodflow Resto, and foodflow Go.

Request method Email support or raise a support request from the app.
Verification We may verify ownership using your registered phone number, email address, or account details.
Completion target Verified requests are generally completed within 30 days.

How To Request Account Deletion

You can request account deletion using either of the following options:

  1. Send an email to support@foodflow.in with the subject line foodflow Account Deletion Request.
  2. Open the foodflow app, go to Profile or Settings, choose Help & Support, and submit a deletion request.

Please include your registered phone number or email address, account type, and any relevant restaurant or partner name.

What Happens After You Request Deletion

  1. We acknowledge the request and may ask for information to verify account ownership.
  2. Once verified, we disable or close the account so it can no longer be used to access foodflow services.
  3. We delete or anonymize personal data that is no longer required for service, legal, security, tax, accounting, dispute, or fraud-prevention purposes.
  4. If there are open orders, unsettled payouts, refunds, chargebacks, support disputes, or legal obligations, deletion may be completed after those items are resolved.

Data We Delete Or Anonymize

  • Profile information such as name, email address, phone number, and profile preferences.
  • Login credentials, authentication tokens, device tokens, and notification preferences.
  • Saved addresses and location preferences where they are not tied to completed transactions or legal records.
  • Restaurant partner profile data, staff access data, menus, and operational settings where deletion is permitted and no active business obligation remains.
  • Support messages and optional user-provided content where retention is not required for an active issue or legal purpose.

Data We May Retain

Some records must be retained for legitimate business, legal, tax, accounting, security, and compliance reasons. Retained records are kept only for as long as necessary and are protected under our data security practices.

  • Order, invoice, payment, payout, refund, tax, and settlement records may be retained for up to 8 years or longer if required by applicable law.
  • Fraud-prevention, abuse-prevention, audit, and security logs may be retained where needed to protect users, partners, and the platform.
  • Support and complaint records may be retained for up to 3 years or for the duration of an active dispute.
  • Backup copies may remain for up to 90 days before being overwritten or securely removed through normal backup cycles.
  • Aggregated or anonymized analytics that cannot reasonably identify you may be retained for service improvement and reporting.

Restaurant Partner And Staff Accounts

If you are a restaurant owner, staff member, or delivery partner, account deletion may affect access to restaurant operations, payouts, order history, tax records, support cases, and compliance documents. Restaurant business records connected to completed orders or settlements may be retained as described above.

Important: Staff access can also be removed by the restaurant owner or authorized administrator. Removing staff access does not necessarily delete historical records required for security, audit, or compliance.

Withdrawal Or Reopening

If you submitted a deletion request by mistake, contact us as soon as possible. Once deletion or anonymization has been completed, the account and deleted data may not be recoverable.
